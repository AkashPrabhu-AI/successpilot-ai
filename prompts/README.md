# AI Prompt Library

## Overview

This folder contains reusable AI prompts designed for SuccessPilot AI features.

The prompts are created to support customer success workflows including customer analysis, onboarding assistance, support automation, and customer risk identification.

---

# Prompt Categories

## 1. Customer Health Analysis

Purpose:

Analyze customer information and identify customer health status.

---

## 2. Customer Onboarding Assistant

Purpose:

Help customers complete onboarding activities.

---

## 3. Support Response Assistant

Purpose:

Generate accurate and helpful customer support responses.

---

## 4. Churn Risk Analysis

Purpose:

Identify customers who may require proactive attention.

---

## 5. Customer Success Recommendations

Purpose:

Generate next-best actions for Customer Success Managers.

---

# Prompt Development Approach

Prompts are designed using:

- Role definition
- Context injection
- Clear instructions
- Output formatting
- Testing and iteration
