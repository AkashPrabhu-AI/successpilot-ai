# SuccessPilot AI Demo Storyboard

## Overview

This storyboard describes the planned product demonstration flow for SuccessPilot AI.

The objective is to showcase how AI helps Customer Success teams understand customer behavior, identify risks, and take proactive actions.

---

# Demo Objective

The demo should demonstrate:

- AI-powered customer intelligence
- Customer health analysis
- AI recommendations
- Customer onboarding assistance
- Business value of AI automation

---

# Scene 1: Product Introduction

## Duration

5-10 seconds

## Screen

SuccessPilot AI landing page.

## User Action

User opens the application.

## Narration

"SuccessPilot AI helps Customer Success teams use Artificial Intelligence to improve customer engagement and retention."

---

# Scene 2: Login and Dashboard

## Duration

10 seconds

## Screen

Customer Success dashboard.

## Show:

- Total customers
- Customer health overview
- Risk indicators
- Recent activities

## User Action

Customer Success Manager views account overview.

## Narration

"Teams get a centralized view of customer health and engagement."

---

# Scene 3: Customer Health Analysis

## Duration

15 seconds

## Screen

Customer profile page.

## User Action

Select customer account and click:

"Analyze with AI"

## AI Output:

- Customer health score
- Customer status
- Risk factors
- Recommended actions

## Narration

"AI analyzes customer data and identifies important signals automatically."

---

# Scene 4: AI Assistant Interaction

## Duration

15 seconds

## Screen

AI chat assistant.

## User Question:

"Why is this customer at risk?"

## AI Response:

- Usage decline
- Negative feedback
- Support issues
- Suggested actions

## Narration

"The AI assistant helps Customer Success Managers make faster decisions."

---

# Scene 5: Customer Onboarding Assistant

## Duration

10 seconds

## Screen

Customer onboarding dashboard.

## Show:

- Completed steps
- Pending activities
- Blockers
- AI recommendations

## Narration

"AI guides customers through onboarding and helps teams identify adoption challenges."

---

# Scene 6: Business Impact Dashboard

## Duration

10 seconds

## Screen

Analytics dashboard.

## Show:

- Improved engagement
- Reduced customer risks
- Customer health trends

## Narration

"SuccessPilot AI enables proactive customer success and better business outcomes."
