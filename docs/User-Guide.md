# User Guide

## Purpose

SuccessPilot AI helps Customer Success Managers automate repetitive tasks using Generative AI.

---

## How to Use

### Step 1: Collect Meeting Notes

Copy meeting notes or upload a meeting transcript.

### Step 2: Generate AI Insights

The AI processes the meeting information and creates:
- Meeting Summary
- Action Items
- Customer Health Analysis
- Follow-up Email

### Step 3: Review the Results

Review the AI-generated content and make any necessary edits.

### Step 4: Share with Customers

Send the follow-up email or update your CRM with the generated insights.

---

## Expected Outcome

- Faster documentation
- Improved customer communication
- Consistent meeting summaries
- Better productivity
