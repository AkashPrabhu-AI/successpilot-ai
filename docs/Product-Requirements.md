# SuccessPilot AI - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Purpose

SuccessPilot AI is a Generative AI-powered Customer Success platform designed to help organizations automate customer onboarding, engagement, support, and retention. The platform enables Customer Success Managers to proactively manage customer relationships using AI-driven insights and workflow automation.

---

# 2. Business Objectives

- Improve customer onboarding efficiency.
- Increase customer engagement.
- Reduce customer churn.
- Automate repetitive Customer Success activities.
- Provide AI-generated recommendations.
- Deliver actionable analytics for decision-making.

---

# 3. Product Vision

To build an intelligent Customer Success platform that combines Artificial Intelligence, automation, and analytics into a single solution that empowers Customer Success teams to scale efficiently.

---

# 4. Target Users

- Customer Success Managers
- Customer Support Executives
- Account Managers
- Implementation Consultants
- Business Leaders
- Operations Managers

---

# 5. User Personas

## Persona 1 – Customer Success Manager

Goals:
- Monitor customer health
- Reduce churn
- Improve adoption
- Track onboarding progress

Pain Points:
- Manual follow-ups
- Lack of customer insights
- Time-consuming reporting

---

## Persona 2 – Customer Support Executive

Goals:
- Resolve customer queries quickly
- Access product knowledge
- Improve response time

Pain Points:
- Repetitive questions
- Knowledge scattered across documents

---

# 6. Core Features

- AI Customer Success Agent
- AI Knowledge Assistant
- Customer Dashboard
- Customer Health Score
- Automated Email Generation
- Workflow Automation
- Analytics & Reporting
- Customer Timeline
- AI Recommendations
- Notifications & Alerts

# 7. User Stories

### US-001

As a Customer Success Manager,

I want to view customer health,

So that I can identify churn risks.

---

### US-002

As a Support Executive,

I want AI to answer product questions,

So that I can resolve tickets faster.

---

### US-003

As an Account Manager,

I want onboarding tasks automated,

So that customers become productive sooner.

---

# 8. MVP Scope

The first release includes:

- User Authentication
- Customer Dashboard
- Customer Profile
- AI Customer Success Agent
- Knowledge Assistant
- Workflow Automation
- Analytics Dashboard
- Notifications

---

# 9. Out of Scope

- Mobile application
- Multi-language support
- Voice AI
- Third-party marketplace integrations

---

# 10. Success Metrics

- 25% reduction in onboarding time
- 20% increase in customer engagement
- 15% reduction in churn
- 90% customer onboarding completion
- CSAT above 90%
- NPS improvement

---

# 11. Risks

- AI hallucinations
- Data privacy concerns
- User adoption challenges
- Integration complexity

---

# 12. Assumptions

- Users have internet access.
- Organizations maintain customer data.
- AI services remain available.
- CRM integrations are configured.
