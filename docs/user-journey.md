# User Journey

## Overview

The user journey describes how users interact with SuccessPilot AI throughout their experience, from account creation to receiving AI-powered customer success insights.

The journey focuses on helping Customer Success teams move from manual customer monitoring to proactive AI-driven engagement.

---

# Journey 1: Customer Success Manager

## Stage 1: Account Access

### User Action

- Logs into SuccessPilot AI platform
- Accesses customer success dashboard

### System Response

- Authenticates user
- Loads organization data
- Displays personalized dashboard

---

## Stage 2: Customer Overview

### User Action

The Customer Success Manager views:

- Customer accounts
- Health scores
- Recent interactions
- Risk indicators

### System Response

AI analyzes available customer information and provides insights.

---

## Stage 3: AI Customer Analysis

### User Action

The user selects a customer account and requests AI analysis.

### AI Processing

The system:

- Reviews customer activity
- Analyzes conversations
- Identifies sentiment
- Detects potential risks

### Output

AI provides:

- Customer health summary
- Risk level
- Key issues
- Recommended actions

---

## Stage 4: Taking Action

### User Action

The Customer Success Manager:

- Creates follow-up tasks
- Contacts customer
- Shares recommendations with team

### System Response

The platform tracks actions and updates customer history.

---

## Stage 5: Continuous Monitoring

The system continuously monitors:

- Customer engagement
- Product usage
- Support interactions
- Feedback

AI generates alerts when risks are detected.

---

# Journey 2: Support Specialist

## Stage 1: Customer Issue Received

User receives a customer query through integrated support platforms.

---

## Stage 2: AI Assistance

AI provides:

- Relevant knowledge articles
- Suggested responses
- Similar past solutions

---

## Stage 3: Resolution

Support specialist resolves the issue and updates customer records.

---

# Journey 3: Business Leader

## Stage 1: Access Analytics Dashboard

Leader views:

- Customer retention trends
- Customer health overview
- AI-generated reports

---

## Stage 2: Strategic Decisions

AI provides insights to help leaders:

- Identify improvement areas
- Reduce churn risk
- Improve customer experience
