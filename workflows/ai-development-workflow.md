# AI Development Workflow

## Overview

The AI development workflow defines the process used to design, test, validate, and improve Artificial Intelligence features in SuccessPilot AI.

The workflow ensures AI solutions are aligned with business goals, provide accurate responses, and continuously improve through evaluation.

# 1. Identify AI Use Case

## Activities

Understand the business problem and define where AI can provide value.

Examples:

- Customer health analysis
- Customer onboarding assistance
- Support response generation
- Churn risk identification

## Output

- AI feature requirement
- Expected business outcome

---

# 2. Define AI Behavior

## Activities

Define how the AI should behave.

Includes:

- AI role definition
- Instructions
- Business context
- Expected response format

Example:


Role:
AI Customer Success Assistant

Goal:
Analyze customer information and provide recommendations.

# 5. AI Testing and Evaluation

## Testing Areas

### Accuracy Testing

Verify:

- Correct information
- Relevant responses
- Useful recommendations

---

### Context Testing

Verify:

- AI understands customer information
- Responses match business scenarios

---

### Safety Testing

Verify:

- No sensitive data exposure
- Proper handling of incorrect requests
